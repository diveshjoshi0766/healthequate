Corporate Sessions


"A Company's success is directly proportional to employee's efficiency and employee's efficiency is directly proportional to their well being."

Sessions with Health Equate will lay the groundwork for a smoother journey at the your workplace. Employers concern towards the well being of the employees results in maximum outcome of their performance and goes a long way towards the company's success.

Our aim is to bring out employees from the state of chronic fatigue or overwhelm and assist in their work life balance. Our goal is to give them inner peace, healthy body & a focused mind that is required for optimal engagement at work or personal zone. Practicing through Health Equate can help in bringing the body and mind into a balanced state.

Our program covers focused fitness and spiritual session which are designed keeping in mind the sedentary lifestyle and stressful work environment of the employees.

What we do/how we do?


Our ninety minute program incorporates

1. Fitness education
2. Guided therapeutic Fitness exercises with postural guidance.
3. Breathing exercise (Ancient breathing exercise)
4. Guided Meditation (Extracts from Twin Hearts)


Why we do?


It has been observed that in companies when individual steps out of their comfort zone to produce more results, then over the period of time, the result comes out at the cost of their health and inner peace. A lot of times corporate lifestyle compromises on self care and fitness activities leading to physical ailments as well as emotional distress. Employees spent most of the time in office and the work load does not permit them to spare time for their health & peace.

Why should you choose us?


We target fitness at a 360 degree level. Our purpose is to bring healthy state inside and outside.

We focus to bring physical and emotional health balance in your life. We also give a complete guide to the employees about the steps that can be taken from their end to keep their mind and body optimally functional.


Our Purpose


We aim to resolve this crucial issue of their life and serve them sessions at their workplace , in their comfort zone, which will bring them health benefits on all level i.e. physical as well as emotional & mental.
