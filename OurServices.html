PHYSICAL THREAPY


Physical Therapy is the healthcare field whic aim to ease pain and help you function, move, and live better. They evaluate and treat physical abnormalities caused due to bad posture, an injury, disease or condition and disability.

Every individual regardless of age, values the ability to function independently in their everyday life. Physical therapy services play a vital role in case of physical impairments associated with injury, disease, , or disorders that interfere with their ability to perform or pursue any number of activities that are necessary or important to them.

WHAT WE AIM


  • ● Relieve pain
    ● Improve movement
    ● Improve strength
    ● Upright Posture
    ● Prevent or recovery from a sports injury
    ● Prevention of disability
    ● Improved Balance
    ● Manage illness like Cardiopulmonary, or arthritis
    ● Recover after pregnancy
    ● Control your bladder and bowel movement

  • People of all ages get physical therapy. It can treat wide variety of medical conditions.
