Meet the mind behind Health Equate






Dr. Neha Gupta is a Physical Therapist , Pranic healer and a yoga instructor. She has a Masters Degree in Musculoskeletal, she has closely worked with patients of all ages and has been using various rehabilitation protocols and intervention on them . She has super specialisation in Mulligan’s concept of manual therapy which strengthens her approach in treating patients with musculoskeletal pathologies.


She is associated with the fitness academy for training fitness instructors and conducting fitness/ postural workout sessions. She practices energy healing therapy in person as well as distantly and has been guiding meditation to masses in order to bring people into a healthy lifestyle and advising them the techniques and ways for better life.







​Dr. Aseem Gupta was a former senior Physiotherapist and Team leader in Artemis hospital and has an experience of 10 years in this field. He has Masters Degree in Sports physiotherapy where he has closely worked with patients with athletic and non athletes population and has devised various rehabilitation protocols and intervention for the same. He has super specialisation in Mulligan’s concept and shoulder rehab which strengthens his approach in treating patients with spine and shoulder pathologies.

He is a member of The Indian association of physiotherapists, India and was associated with the sports authority of India as a part of the core team. DrAseem Gupta’s skilled approach in treatment of varied kinds of Musculoskeletal, Cardiac, Neuro and other rehabilitation fields has made him a known name.
