OUR SERVICES


Our specifically designed therapeutic workout sessions are the fundamental part of the physical therapy services . Ultimate goal of a therapeutic exercise program is the achievement of an optimal level of symptom- free movement during basic to complex physical activities.

Our Therapeutic workout session includes a wide range of physical activities that focuses on restoration and maintenance of movement, flexibility, strength, endurance and stability of weak and ailing parts of your body. Our goal is to bring you a fully functioning, pain-free state and healthy lifestyle.

Our group session includes therapeutic exercises which are designed according to your condition in order to promote your healing and improve your functioning. We will assess you by performing functional tests to know your condition and group you with the people suffering from similar conditions.

Our therapeutic workout includes:


● Mobility exercises
● Flexibility enhancing exercise
● Progressive resistive exercise
● Balance training
● Strength training
● Aerobic conditioning

Our physical therapist will teach you the correct postural pattern. Good posture helps you look good and confident. It helps you to develop strength, flexibility, and good balance. In turn it leads to reduced muscle pain and improved energy level throughout the day. Proper posture reduces stress and strain on muscles and ligaments, and thus prevents injuries.
